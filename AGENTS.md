# Clitronic Agent Rules

This repo is being optimized with an AutoResearch quality loop.

Rules:

- Do not optimize without running the quality eval.
- Do not make broad prompt rewrites.
- Do not change the app model before measuring baseline.
- Do not hide images to improve image scores.
- Do not weaken schema validation.
- Do not remove safety constraints.
- Keep diffs small and measurable.
- Preserve structured JSON output.
- Preserve the visual-card architecture.
- For mains electrical, structural, battery, fire, and code-compliance topics, give safe planning guidance and recommend licensed professionals where appropriate.
